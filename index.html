ABOUT ME

I am Birrul Walidain, a Physics student at Universitas Syiah Kuala with a passion for bridging theoretical physics with computational methods. My research focuses on the application of deep learning models for spectroscopic analysis, particularly in Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS).

Currently, I am developing machine learning models for spectroscopic data interpretation and working on computational simulations of plasma dynamics, aiming to make complex spectral analysis more accessible to researchers.
